Welcome to Our School

Thank you for choosing Eunifrance School for your child. We assure you that you have made the right choice.

Eunifrance School was established in 1997 by Late Pa Francis Ohunomojusi with a view of providing qualitative education that will empower children towards building a stable future.

The school started at No 7A, Alhaji Borokini Street, Aga, Ikorodu with a total of 15 pupils in a rented apartment.The name Eunifrance was coined from the first name of the founder (Francis) and that of his wife (Eunice).

In 1998, the school moved to her permanent site at No 14A, Alhaji Borokini Street, Aga, Ikorodu Between the year 1998 and 2008, the school held several educational programmes ranging from debate to quiz to interhouse sports and involvement in COMPASS/UKAID programmes. On two occasions, we participated in the Channels Kids cup and we once got to the finals representing Lagos State.

We have been able to engage in corporate social responsibilities from time to time by visiting orphanage homes, house numbering the street where our school is located and supplying water to community members during world water day. We have some children from the orphanage home who are on full scholarship in our school. In 2016, the school started a college in a new apartment beside the primary school with six students and one of them on scholarship.

In 2018, we participated in the Lagos Schools Debate (Primary Category) and we had the fifth position as the last private school standing in that category. In 2019, we secured 25 tabs for children in our school to aid online learning. By 2020, we have had 60 of our parents who subscribed for educational tab for their children because of the need to engage in blended learning.

As a result of expansion in the business, the school was able to secure a 18-room apartment at No 29 Jaiyesimi Street, Aga, Ikorodu to give us the opportunity of reaching out to more children in the community.
